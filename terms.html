Terms of Service

1. Order Process

  • Orders must be placed at least 5–7 days in advance.
  • Provide full details (cake size, flavor, design, date, etc.).
  • We accept inspiration photos, but exact replication is not guaranteed.

2. Payment Terms

  • 50% down payment required to confirm your order.
  • Remaining balance due upon pickup or before delivery.
  • Accepted payments: GCash / Bank Transfer / COD

3. Cancellations & Refunds

  • Cancellations 3+ days before due date may be refunded (minus fees).
  • No refunds for same-day or last-minute cancellations.
  • Full refund if we are unable to fulfill your order due to emergencies.

4. Changes to Orders

  • Minor changes allowed up to 3 days before due date.
  • Major changes may not be accommodated after confirmation.

5. Pickup & Delivery

  • Be on time for pickup — cakes are perishable.
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the delivery.
  • We are not liable for damages once the cake is picked up/delivered.

6. Allergies & Storage

  • Cakes may contain allergens (e.g., dairy, eggs, nuts).
  • Please inform us of any allergies before placing an order.
  • Store cakes properly (refrigeration for cream-based cakes recommended).
